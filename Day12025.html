One-day winter itinerary through Munich

Breakfast at A Little Lost

Start with a warm coffee and a simple breakfast. Keep it short and light — the day will be cold.

Tram ride north

Take the tram heading north. Stay warm, enjoy the quiet ride, and watch the city go by under winter light.

Swim at Dantebad

Arrive at Dantebad for a winter swim. The heated outdoor pool is open even in the cold months — a good way to wake up fully.

Rest at Pflegerstraße

After swimming, stop at Pflegerstraße. Rest, warm up, and take it easy before the evening.

Cinema: “Das Kanu des Manitu”

Finish the day at the cinema. Watch Das Kanu des Manitu and end the evening indoors, away from the cold.
